Delete Yourself

Individual Project

My Role

Designer

Developer

A website dedicated to helping visitors practice good online habits, as well as taking control of their online presence

Purpose

Delete Yourself was made as a final project for a class during fall quarter of 2017. The project assignment was to create a website that revolved around a social justice issue, or having some sort of social impact. I chose to focus on online safety, as technology is continuing to advance at an astounding rate, but that doesn't mean peoples' general understanding of it is on the same level The intent was to inform visitors about how to stay safe online as well as other related aspects, such as privacy on social media and securing mobile devices.
